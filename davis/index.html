Davis Residence

Wellsley, MA

The new living room is graciously proportioned with seating for six, as well as a grand piano. Built in cabinetry conceals the entertainment electronics, and incorporates a place for alabaster wall sconces that provide warmth and character. Recessed lighting in the ceiling has been carefully designed for reading, and the detailed ceiling moldings enhance the formal geometry of the room. A southern exposure provides warmth and views to the new backyard garden.
